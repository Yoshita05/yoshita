Tea Box - Package Package Design

Express Tea is a fictional brand that manufactures authentic Indian Tea that flavours with Indian spices. The Express Tea is available in different flavours namely - Black Tea, Yellow Tea, White Tea, Green Tea and Red Tea.
The idea behind making the package cover was to increase the understanding of elements and placing on the package cover. Moreover, it enhanced to develop production knowledge and technical skills through the development of final package art with rendering in Illustrator.
The final deliverables included the production ready illustrator files with dielines and bleed, three dimensional rendering in Illustrator.

Software Used

Adobe Indesign, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator
